Declaration of Independence
The most important document of Early American literature.

Claim

Happiness and Freedom

The Declaration of Independence effectively sums up centuries worth of sentiments surrounding happiness and freedom, notably seen in its incorporation of themes common to Puritan and non-Puritan works, even including Native American values that existed long before the arrival of the English.

Support

Puritan values of happiness

John Winthrop's "Dreams of a City on a Hill"
